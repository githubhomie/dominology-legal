Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start an online game? Tap Play, then Start Game. Choose your game settings and share the join code with your opponent.
Do I need an account to play? Yes, an account is required to use Dominology. You can sign up quickly with Apple, Google, or email.
How do tournaments work? Create a tournament, share the join code, and once everyone joins the lobby, start the bracket. The app handles matchups and advancement automatically.
How is my rating calculated? Dominology uses an ELO-style rating system. Win against higher-rated players to climb faster. Your rating only changes in Rated games, not Casual.
How do I delete my account? Go to Profile, tap the menu (three dots), and select Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
