Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2025

Dominology ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dominoes score tracking application. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (if using email sign-in)
  • Display name
  • Profile information from Apple or Google (if using those sign-in methods)

Game Data

  • Game scores and history
  • Player names and statistics
  • Tournament information

Social Features

  • Friends list
  • Friend requests
  • Usernames

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app. This includes:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the score tracking service
  • To sync your games across devices
  • To enable multiplayer and social features
  • To improve the app experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be processed by:

  • Firebase (Google) - for authentication, database, and analytics
  • Apple - if using Sign in with Apple

Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes your data.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Contact us with questions

Children's Privacy

Dominology is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
